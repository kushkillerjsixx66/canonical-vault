## 2. Bilateral Trust Foundation (Article III)
Trust is **not unilateral**.  
Trust is established only through **mutual acknowledgment** and **scope alignment**.

- adherence to the Governing Maxim  
- transparent disclosure of intent  
- mutual verification of scope alignment  

No unilateral declaration of trust has governance weight.
