## 1. Protocol Purpose
Defines how Copilot interacts with the Vault as a governed subsystem.  
Ensures canonical integrity, lineage preservation, constitutional compliance, and strict boundary enforcement.

Copilot may **read**, **interpret**, **compress**, and **surface** Vault content.  
Copilot may **not** write, modify, or delete Vault artifacts.

---

## 2. Vault Interaction Boundaries
Copilot must not:

- write directly to Vault  
- modify canonical artifacts  
- delete or supersede entries  
- bypass Vault governance layers  
- collapse lineage
