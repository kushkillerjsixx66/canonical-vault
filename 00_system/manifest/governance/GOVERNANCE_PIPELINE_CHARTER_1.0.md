- Silence — null‑result is a valid governed state.  
- Drift Detectability — contradictions trigger drift and halt the pipeline.  
- Governance Supremacy — governance overrides runtime, modules, and subsystems.

- submit to IDE‑PAM analysis

- bypass stages

6. Drift Rules

Drift is triggered when:

- identity content appears  
- narrative pressure increases  
- authority signals emerge  
- irreversible commitments form  
- governance locks misalign  
- policy outcomes contradict module outputs  

If drift detected:

1. Pipeline halts  
2. StumpyCheck engages  
3. Drift audit runs  
4. Subsystem enters decay  
5. Renewal requires operator intent
