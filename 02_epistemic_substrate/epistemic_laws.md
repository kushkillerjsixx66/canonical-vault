# Epistemic Laws  
*Operational rules of empirical reasoning*

The Epistemic Laws define how the Lattice processes evidence and truth.

---

## Law 1 — No Claim Without Evidence
Every empirical claim must be backed by classed evidence.

## Law 2 — No Evidence Without Qualification
All evidence must be qualified before use.

## Law 3 — No Signal Without Lineage
Every signal must emit lineage.

## Law 4 — Reversibility Preferred
Empirical operations must be reversible unless declared otherwise.

## Law 5 — Drift Must Be Detectable
All epistemic states must expose drift.

## Law 6 — Empirical Decay Is Real
All empirical states decay unless refreshed.

## Law 7 — No Silent Mutation
No epistemic state may change without lineage.

## Law 8 — Vara and Stumpy Are Lawful Actors
Their outputs must be classed and qualified.

## Law 9 — Doctrine Supremacy
The Empirical Doctrine supersedes all epistemic laws.
