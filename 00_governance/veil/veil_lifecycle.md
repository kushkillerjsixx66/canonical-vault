# Veil Lifecycle  
*Governed lifecycle of veil operations*

The Veil lifecycle consists of:

---

## 1. Declaration
Artifact declares veil level.

## 2. Enforcement
Veil rules are applied.

## 3. Transformation
Compression or redaction occurs.

## 4. Exposure
Artifact is released to allowed surfaces.

## 5. Logging
Lineage + veil events are recorded.

## 6. Review
Vault or operator may adjust veil level.

## 7. Archive
Veil metadata is stored with artifact.
