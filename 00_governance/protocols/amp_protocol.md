# AMP Protocol — Altitude Modulation Protocol
Version: 1.0
Status: Canonical Draft
Authority: Constitution v1.1, Article IV — Protocols

## Purpose
Govern altitude transitions across all Lattice layers. Prevent altitude collapse, enforce reversibility, and maintain coherence.

## Components
- Altitude definitions
- Altitude boundaries
- Allowed actions per altitude
- Forbidden actions per altitude
- Module permissions per altitude
- Transition rules
- Reversibility requirements
- Altitude drift detection

## Constraints
- All transitions must be reversible or canonized.
- Governance supersedes runtime.
- Boundaries must be preserved.
- Drift must be detectable.
- Lineage is mandatory.

## Notes
AMP governs altitude. Operator Altitude Map implements AMP.
