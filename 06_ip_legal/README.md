# 06_ip_legal  
*IP, licensing, attribution, and legal envelope for the Lattice*

The IP & Legal layer defines:

- ownership and authorship  
- licensing models  
- attribution obligations  
- third-party usage rules  
- legal constraints and lineage  

It ensures that the Lattice operates:

- lawfully  
- transparently  
- with clear ownership  
- with explicit licensing  
- with correct attribution  

This layer binds the **legal reality** of the system to its **governance reality**.
