# Licensing Policy  
*Licensing model for code, content, and artifacts*

---

## 1. Licensing Classes

Artifacts are licensed under one of:

- **Internal‑Only** — for Lattice internal use only  
- **Source‑Available** — visible, but with restricted rights  
- **Open‑Licensed** — under a standard open license (e.g., MIT, Apache‑2.0, CC‑BY)  
- **Custom License** — bespoke terms for specific artifacts  

---

## 2. Default Licensing

- Governance artifacts: **Internal‑Only** by default  
- System specs: **Source‑Available** by default  
- Runtime code (if any): **Open‑Licensed** or **Source‑Available**, per repo decision  
- Narrative artifacts: **Custom License** or **CC‑BY** (operator‑chosen)

---

## 3. License Declaration

- Every public artifact must declare:
  - license type  
  - rights granted  
  - restrictions  
  - attribution requirements  

- License declarations must be:
  - human‑readable  
  - machine‑detectable (where possible)  

---

## 4. Compatibility

- Third‑party licenses must be compatible with:
  - chosen open licenses  
  - internal policies  
  - legal constraints  

- Incompatible licenses must not be integrated.

---

## 5. Purpose

This policy ensures:

- explicit licensing  
- predictable reuse  
- legal compatibility
