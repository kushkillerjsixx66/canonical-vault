# IP & Legal Index  
*Ownership, licensing, attribution, and legal envelopes for the Lattice*

The IP & Legal layer defines:

- who owns what  
- how artifacts may be used  
- how third-party material is handled  
- how attribution is tracked  
- what legal constraints bind the system  

This index is the entry point into all IP & Legal documents.

---

## Core Documents

- **ownership_policy.md** — ownership and authorship rules  
- **licensing_policy.md** — licensing model for code, content, and artifacts  
- **attribution_register.yaml** — registry of attribution obligations  
- **third_party_register.yaml** — registry of third‑party assets and terms  
- **ip_legal_constraints.yaml** — non‑negotiable legal constraints  
- **ip_legal_events.log** — append‑only legal lineage log  
- **README.md** — overview of the IP & Legal layer

## Purpose

The IP & Legal layer ensures:

- clear ownership  
- explicit licensing  
- correct attribution  
- lawful use of third‑party assets  
- traceable legal decisions  

It is the **legal envelope** of the Lattice.
