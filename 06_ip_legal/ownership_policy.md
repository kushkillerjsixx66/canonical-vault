# Ownership Policy  
*Authorship, control, and rights in the Lattice*

---

## 1. Sovereign Ownership

- The sovereign operator **JRM‑01 (@liminaljermo)** is the primary rights holder for:  
  - governance artifacts  
  - system specifications  
  - operator manual  
  - epistemic substrate  
  - Vault Pipeline design  
  - runtime and module architecture

- Sovereign ownership is:
  - non‑delegable  
  - lineage‑anchored  
  - constitutionally recognized  

---

## 2. Artifact Classes

Artifacts are classed as:

- **Governance Artifacts** — constitutions, protocols, invariants  
- **System Artifacts** — specs, models, contracts, lifecycles  
- **Runtime Artifacts** — logs, lineage, events  
- **Narrative Artifacts** — posts, docs, surfaces  

Each class may have distinct licensing and attribution rules (see licensing_policy.md).

---

## 3. Authorship Rules

- Authorship must be declared where feasible.  
- Co‑authorship must be explicit.  
- Derived works must reference their source.  
- Operator intent governs authorship disputes.

---

## 4. Transfer & Assignment

- Ownership transfer requires:
  - explicit operator intent  
  - written record  
  - lineage entry  

- Default: no transfer without explicit declaration.

---

## 5. Purpose

This policy ensures:

- clarity of ownership  
- stable authorship  
- lawful reuse and extension
