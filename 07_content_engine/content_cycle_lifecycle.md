# Content Cycle Lifecycle  
*Governed weekly content cycle*

The weekly cycle consists of:

---

## 1. Ingest
Signals + prior narrative enter the engine.

---

## 2. Mechanism Extraction (Monday)
Mechanism is derived from epistemic + narrative inputs.

---

## 3. Threshold Definition (Tuesday)
Tensions, thresholds, and operator questions are defined.

---

## 4. Workflow Operationalization (Wednesday)
Mechanism becomes operator workflow.

---

## 5. Theory Expansion (Thursday)
Mechanism is abstracted into conceptual insight.

---

## 6. Field INTEL Interpretation (Friday)
Audience + analytics signals are interpreted.

---

## 7. Narrative Formation (Sunday)
Narrative closes the loop and seeds next cycle.

---

## 8. Archive + Seed
Artifacts enter Vault Pipeline.  
Next cycle seeds are generated.
